Project Proposal

Maebh Thomas: Maebh's Urbanity

An informative organic campain-based city builder

Rational:

During this cource I have acquired and more importantly developed many diverse design-related skillsets and made sure to also focus on these same skills and software packages from a more development-related angle that I was more previosly expreienced with. I now found I am most engaged and uniquely skilled in the intersection of art/design and technical apects of grathics, especially involving many different diciplins in one progect. In terms of as-close-to-tradional-as-posible positions that puts me between the enigeneer position of a grathics programer or the artist position of technical artist. It also makes a good basis for indie development. This basis of understanding of what engages me and skills to develop thurver is what has been most educational. In my FMP, I whould like to showcase my skills for studio and indie positions and provide a tested base concept to be used for a commecially viable solo indie project.

Project Concept:

I want to make the following deliverables:

  • A sizable demo for an informative organic campaign-based city-builder (web/desktop app),
  • assets for a steam page (animated gifs + 2D art)

It should to be the following:

  • Accessible and entertaining to a range of demographics not familiar with city-builder concepts,
  • showing diverse urban, architectural and transportational forms,
  • playable on most contemporary hardware,
  • teaching modern sustainable planning practices and understanding.

I will use the following:

  • A ECS game framework on
  • a live linux development environment,
  • our college texture library,
  • Photoshop and CSP for 2D manipulation with college graphics tablets for some assets which can be fitted into the time I have with them,
  • in department and online friend-group informal discussion and forms.

Evaluation:

I will take feedback at the start, end and throughout through informal discussions and forms in the classroom and in a more varied friend group, though it is still a relatively small age (16-19), location (pretty much just Londoners) and social outlook (all left-wing) variety. I will attempt to get more varied feedback sources in my final evaluation as one of my aims is to be accessible to a large demographic variety, however doing this throughout would be too costly as it requires specifically seeking and overseeing testers.

I will seek comparision to profesional published games as I want to aim to showcase features that are genuinly inovitive, while accepting I cannot create a larger or universally supparsing (even demo of ) product.

I will record thought making processes in a notebook and technical changes and reasoning behind them will be recorded in Git version control with automatic enforcing that I justify any change to future me.
