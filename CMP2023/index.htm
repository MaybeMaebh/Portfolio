Explanation

On this site I will document the process of making a theorectical submission for the artstation competition.

I chose the Cities of the Future breif as I have a strong passion for atypical city planning as is fitting to future city ideas. I especially want to experiment wsith more sustanible ways of living and realistic solourpunk ashpetics.

I also want to develop my 3D skills with a focus on technical art and to create a peice of work for my portfolio.

Planned Deliverables

  • 2 4K renders showing the landscape of the city from different angles,
  • A 4K render showing a view a street scape at predestrian level,
  • An animated video render of the transport system
  • A stylised poster render advertising the city and
  • Basic posters and navigation aids used in the city.

Current Compleated Deliverables

  • An animated video render deminstrating procedural systems
  • A navigation aid used in the city.

Though I only compleated 2 far more basic deliverables, and ended up having far fewer usable workdays than typically allocated to the CMP, I feel I was able to get what I needed to out of it.

I was able to learn a new software package, programing language and gain undersanding of the basics of procedural environment generation, all of which are crucial towards the skills needed for technical art.
