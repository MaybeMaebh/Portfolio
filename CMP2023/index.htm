Explanation

On this site I will document the process of making a theorectical submission for the artstation competition.

I chose the Cities of the Future breif as I have a strong passion for atypical city planning as is fitting to future city ideas. I especially want to experiment wsith more sustanible ways of living and realistic solourpunk ashpetics.
